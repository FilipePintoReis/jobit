The Feedzai Story

Feedzai was founded by data scientists and aerospace engineers with one powerful mission: Make commerce safe for business customers and create a better experience for their consumers through artificially intelligent machine learning. The mightiest companies in the world use Feedzai’s anti-fraud technology to keep commerce moving safely.

Our DNA

Many years back, our team journey began in university research and at the European Space Agency, Visa, PayPal, Amazon, Ayasdi and Experian. Right now, we’re propelling all that DNA forward and towards building a big platform to speed the business of risk management into the future. Everyday, we’re re-imagining financial technology and making it better for everyone. Transparency, speed and efficiency—these are the expectations for the new generation of buyers, sellers and anyone looking to connect over commerce. Join us as we create the leading machine-learning platform for keeping commerce safe. It’s going to be an exciting journey.
