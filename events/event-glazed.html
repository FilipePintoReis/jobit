Pedro Campos

Pedro Campos, 33, is founder and manager of the companies Glazed - Elite Developers and Switch Payments. He finished in 2008 the Integrated Master in Informatics and Computer Engineering of FEUP, passing through the University of Bristol. After working as a Freelancer for a London startup, as an Information Retrieval Researcher, and as a frontend programmer for a professional video software company, he decides to devote himself to his own projects. In 2009, he was one of the founding members of blip, leading the mobile development area. In 2011, he founded Glazed - Elite Developers, a mobile and web software development company. In 2013, he founded Cashtag, a peer-to-peer payments app invested by Seedcamp. In 2014, he founded Switch Payments, an international payment processor that processes millions of transactions for some of the world's largest e-commerce. He has led the development teams for several other projects such as MEOMusic at nMusic, or the black-and-white project at Farfetch.
